Here are some fun facts about Venezuela!

Angel Falls: Venezuela is home to Angel Falls (Salto Ángel), the tallest waterfall in the world, with a height of 3,212 feet.

Arepas: Venezuela’s national dish, arepas, is a versatile cornmeal flatbread often stuffed with cheese, meat, or vegetables.

Hallacas: During Christmas, Venezuelans enjoy hallacas, a traditional dish made with corn dough stuffed with meat, wrapped in banana leaves, and boiled.

Sports: Baseball is the most popular sport in Venezuela. Players such as Ronald Acuña Jr. and Miguel Cabrera came out of Venezuela.
